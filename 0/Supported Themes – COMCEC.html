Supported Themes

The COMCEC Coordination Office (CCO) provides financial support to the projects in 6 different cooperation areas as agriculture, finance, poverty alleviation, transport and communication, tourism and trade. The CCO determines sectoral themes under each cooperation area by considering outputs of the Working Groups as well as policy recommendations adopted by the COMCEC Ministerial Sessions. In order to be eligible for COMCEC Project Funding, projects should be designed with respect to one of these sectoral themes.
